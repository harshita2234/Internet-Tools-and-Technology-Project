MAHE Manipal awards merit scholarship for the following Post Graduate (PG) and Super Speciality (SS) programs
  • Post Graduate (PG)
    • Doctor of Medicine (MD) at Kasturba Medical College (KMC) Manipal
    • Doctor of Medicine (MD) at Kasturba Medical College (KMC) Mangalore
    • Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) at Manipal College of Dental Sciences (MCODS) Manipal
    • Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) at Manipal College of Dental Sciences (MCODS) Mangalore
  • Super Specialty (SS)
    • Doctorate of Medicine (DM) at Kasturba Medical College (KMC) Manipal
    • Master of Chirurgiae (MCh) at Kasturba Medical College (KMC) Manipal

Scholarship Guidelines:

  • Eligibility Requirements
    • Program Eligibility
    MD Pass in MBBS degree from an Institution / University recognized by National Medical Council (NMC) / erstwhile Medical Council of India (MCI)
    MDS Pass in BDS degree from an Institution / University recognized by Dental Council of India (DCI)
    DM Pass in MD / DNB from required specialization * from an Institution / University recognized by National Medical Council (NMC) / erstwhile Medical Council of India (MCI)
    MCh Pass in MD / DNB from required specialization * from an Institution / University recognized by National Medical Council (NMC) / erstwhile Medical Council of India (MCI)

    # 100% scholarships available only for 2 seats based on first come first serve merit basis

    • Candidates are required to pay the full first year course fee + Rs. 10,000 refundable Caution Deposit at the time of Reporting
    • The candidate will have to take care of other expenses such as hostel accommodation, food, books etc.
    • The excess amount paid (after applying the scholarship) shall be refunded within 2 months of completion of all rounds of Counseling, only on submission of duly filled MAHE Scholarship Application Form 2024 to the MAHE Manipal Admissions office.
    • The award of scholarship for the subsequent years will be the same as given, at the time of admission in the first year.
    • Scholarship will be extended for the subsequent year only to those students who qualifies in the year-end examinations as per NMC (erstwhile MCI) / DCI guidelines
    • Scholarship will be forfeited in the instances of irregularity in attendance, proven charges of indiscipline, bringing disrepute to the Department / Institution / University etc
    • In case the candidate leaves the course before the course completion (for any reason), he / she MUST reimburse the total course fee (as per Prospectus 2024), back to MAHE Manipal before leaving the program
